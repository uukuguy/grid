## Decisions Made

- **EventSink service architecture**: New `EventSink` gRPC service (not bidirectional RuntimeService.EmitEvent) to maintain clean client/server asymmetry. L1 = gRPC client, L4 = gRPC server.
- **OPTIONAL contract tier**: EventSink matches Health/PauseSession tier — no comparison runtime forced to implement. Best-effort augmentation; primary persistence remains file/SQLite sinks.
- **ADR number deviation**: Plan specified ADR-V2-029 but that ID was already occupied by "Engine vs Data/Integration Boundary Contract" (Phase 5.5, Accepted 2026-05-22). Used ADR-V2-033 (next clear number, V2-030 reserved by V2-029).
- **Terminate idempotency**: NO-OP for second Terminate call eliminates retry-loop bugs in orchestrator retry logic.
- **NOT_FOUND vs FAILED_PRECONDITION**: gRPC-standard distinction — NOT_FOUND means "resource doesn't exist", FAILED_PRECONDITION means "resource exists but in wrong state."
